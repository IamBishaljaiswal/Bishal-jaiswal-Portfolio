C#- Project- Belfield Service Center-2020

Belfield Service Centre (BSC), is an NSW based auto repair Centre which takes a classic old car and makes it look good. From the exterior to the engine, transmission and chassis, from major servicing to replacing a simple part, it does it all. The company policy is to provide outstanding service to customers. BSC had been providing vehicle inspection and preventative maintenance to its customers for a long time ago. They offer the highest quality customer service and will treat their automobile as if it is their own. In addition, there are a wide variety of problems that can occur with the vehicle, and Belfield Service Centre has the proper equipment and experience to locate, repair and/or replace the issue quickly and effectively. The Preventative Maintenance services provide maintenance service to ensure customer vehicle is safe and sound to protect them and their family. This project aims to create cloud-based (AWS) order management system that will be scalable and easy to use as per the need of the Belfield Service Centre.
